\CharSect{ABILITIES/TALENTS}

\begin{itemize}[noitemsep]
    \item\textbf{Forceful Personality:} Once per session, you may spend a story point as an incidental.  If you do so, on your next skill check, double the amount of strain you inflict or heal (choose before making the check).
    \item\textbf{Finesse:} You may use Agility rather than Brawn for Brawl and Melee checks.
        \item\textbf{Clever Retort:} Once per encounter, when you are targeted by a social check, you may add \Threat\Threat\spa to the dice pool before it is rolled.
        \item\textbf{Inspiring Rhetoric:} As an action, you may make an \textbf{Average (\df\df) Leadership} check.  For each \Success, one ally within short range heals one strain.  For each \Advantage, one benefitting ally heals an additional strain.
\end{itemize}
